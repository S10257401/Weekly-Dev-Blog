Week 6

  • Understanding hoisting and how variables declared with var are hoisted to the top of the function or global scope, while variables declared with let and const are not hoisted.
  • Learning about the let keyword, which is block-scoped and does not allow redeclaration of variables within the same scope.
  • Understanding constants declared with const, which cannot be reassigned after initialization, but their properties can be modified if they are objects.
  • Exploring the for...of statement, which iterates over iterable objects such as arrays, maps, sets, strings, and typed arrays, providing a concise way to loop through elements.
  • Recap of literal objects, function-based objects, and the usage of the for...of loop.
  • Emphasizing the importance of practice and using browser developer tools/debugger for debugging and learning.
