Week 1

  • Learned about HTML tables and their structure, including <table>, </tr>, </th>, and </td> tags for organizing tabular data.
  • Explored the use of HTML <iframe> tag to embed another document within the current HTML document, commonly used for embedding rich media resources like YouTube videos.
  • Applied knowledge by creating a recipe card challenge, incorporating embedded video resources, and using tables to showcase additional information.
  • Discussed the characteristics of a good website, including usability, usefulness, attractiveness, and stickiness, and analyzed examples like Lingscars.com to understand the elements that contribute to good or poor website design.
  • Engaged in team discussions to share insights on favorite websites and what makes them appealing, culminating in a group presentation highlighting key points about website quality.
